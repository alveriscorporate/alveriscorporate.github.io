GENERAL QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to establish a company in the UAE?

The timeframe depends on the chosen jurisdiction, business activity and the completeness of the required documentation. Our consultants manage the process efficiently and keep you informed throughout every stage.

What is the difference between a Mainland company and a Free Zone company?

Mainland companies generally offer greater flexibility to operate throughout the UAE, while Free Zone companies provide attractive benefits for businesses targeting international trade or specific industries. The most suitable option depends on your business objectives.

Can foreign investors own 100% of a company in the UAE?

Many business activities now permit 100% foreign ownership, although the applicable regulations vary depending on the jurisdiction and nature of the business. We will advise you on the most suitable structure for your objectives.

Which business licence should I choose?

The appropriate licence depends on your intended business activities. Common licence categories include commercial, professional and industrial licences. Our consultants will recommend the option that best matches your operations.

Can Alveris manage the entire company formation process?

Yes. We provide end-to-end assistance including business setup, licence applications, government approvals, visa processing, compliance and ongoing corporate support.

Do you assist with UAE residence visas?

Yes. We assist eligible business owners and employees with visa applications, Emirates ID procedures and related government formalities as part of our corporate services.

Is a physical office required to establish a company?

Office requirements vary depending on the chosen jurisdiction and licence type. Some business structures offer flexible workspace solutions while others require dedicated office premises.

Can I change my business activities after incorporation?

Yes. Subject to the applicable regulations, business activities and licences can usually be amended. We can assist with the necessary applications and approvals.

Do I need to maintain accounting records for my business?

Yes. Maintaining accurate accounting records is an important business responsibility in the UAE. Proper bookkeeping supports financial management, regulatory compliance and informed business decision-making.

Who is required to register for UAE Corporate Tax?

Corporate Tax obligations depend on your business activities, legal structure and applicable regulations. We assist businesses with registration, compliance and ongoing Corporate Tax requirements.

Do you provide VAT registration and filing services?

Yes. We assist eligible businesses with VAT registration, VAT return preparation, compliance reviews and ongoing VAT advisory services.

Can you manage bookkeeping on a monthly basis?

Absolutely. Our bookkeeping services include transaction recording, bank reconciliations, financial reporting and ongoing accounting support tailored to your business.

Do you provide payroll management services?

Yes. We offer payroll processing, employee record management, WPS support and payroll reporting to help businesses meet their employment obligations efficiently.

Can Alveris help my business remain compliant?

Yes. We assist businesses with licence renewals, regulatory filings, accounting obligations, taxation requirements and other compliance matters to help reduce administrative burden.

Do you provide business advisory and management consulting?

Yes. Our consultants work with entrepreneurs and established businesses to improve operational efficiency, strengthen business performance and support sustainable long-term growth.

Do you continue supporting clients after company formation?

Absolutely. Our relationship with clients extends beyond incorporation. We provide ongoing accounting, taxation, compliance, PRO services and strategic business support as your business grows.

Can you assist businesses that are already operating in the UAE?

Yes. We support both newly established and existing businesses with accounting, taxation, compliance, restructuring, operational improvements and business advisory services.

How do I get started with Alveris Corporate Services?

Simply contact us by telephone, email, WhatsApp or by completing the enquiry form on our Contact page. We'll arrange an initial consultation to understand your business objectives and recommend the most suitable solution.
